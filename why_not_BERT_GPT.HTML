"For the ServiceNow integration, we used a combination of NLP techniques. We employed Named Entity Recognition (NER) with spaCy to extract key entities like device names, error codes, and affected services from incident reports. For intent classification, we fine-tuned a BERT model on our internal IT support tickets to categorize issues accurately. However, for remedy generation, we needed a model with deep domain knowledge and strong language generation capabilities. We chose Megatron-LM over GPT-3 or BERT because it's pre-trained on a massive corpus of IT troubleshooting data, making it more specialized for our needs."
