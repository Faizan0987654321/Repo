"Fine-tuning Megatron-LM involved several steps. First, we used transfer learning, starting with the model pre-trained on IT forums, academic papers, and manual databases. Then, we fine-tuned it on over 100,000 historical ServiceNow tickets where the remedies were known to be successful. We also incorporated reinforcement learning, using IT staff feedback as rewards to optimize for accuracy and safety. A key challenge was maintaining coherence in long-form solutions, which we addressed using beam search and top-k sampling during text generation."
